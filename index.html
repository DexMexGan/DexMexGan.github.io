About Me

Hi guys, my name is Axel Encalada-Arce, I started this whole photography journey on a whim.

I bought my first camera in 2018 during black friday. It was a Nikon D3500 DSLR.

Initially I just use it to take pictures during vacations or family gatherings.

However I went to this powerlifitng meet one time, and I took my camera.

After reviewing the photos I took at the meet, I thought the picutres looked fine, but

they definitly could look a lot better. I then started to practice how to take photos manually

After hit the cap on the Nikon I decided to make an invesment in the Sony A7 IV.

Now I try and take photos whenever I can and managed to get gigs for photoshoots.
